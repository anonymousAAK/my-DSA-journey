Line Segment Intersection

WEEK 27 - WEB (JS) ADVANCED TOPICS

CONCEPT: Two segments AB and CD intersect iff endpoints straddle each other in cross-product orientation, or one endpoint lies on the other segment (collinear case).

KEY POINTS: ccw() orientation; four o1..o4 signs; on-segment fallback; O(1) per query.

ALGORITHM: compute o1..o4; proper intersection when signs differ on both pairs; else check on-segment fallbacks.

JS-SPECIFIC NOTES: JS doubles work; for large integer coordinates consider BigInt to avoid floating error.

DRY RUN: AB=(0,0)-(4,4), CD=(0,4)-(4,0) -> intersects at (2,2).

COMPLEXITY: Time O(1); Space O(1).
