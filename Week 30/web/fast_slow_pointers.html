Fast & Slow Pointers (Floyd's Tortoise & Hare)

WEEK 30 - WEB (JS) ADVANCED TOPICS

CONCEPT: Two pointers at speeds 1 and 2; meeting implies a cycle. Generalises to any deterministic next-state function (Happy Number).

KEY POINTS: O(n) time, O(1) space; for cycle entry reset one cursor to head and walk both 1-step.

ALGORITHM: hasCycle, detectCycleStart, middle node, isHappy.

JS-SPECIFIC NOTES: JS objects/classes work fine for ListNode; identity compare with === checks references.

DRY RUN: 1->2->3->2 cycle: hasCycle=true, start val=2. isHappy(19)=true; isHappy(2)=false.

COMPLEXITY: Time O(n); Space O(1).
