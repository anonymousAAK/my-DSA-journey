Two Pointers Pattern

WEEK 30 - WEB (JS) ADVANCED TOPICS

CONCEPT: Two indices traverse an array (often sorted) under monotonic rules, giving O(n) or O(n log n) algorithms.

KEY POINTS: on sorted input, sum a[l]+a[r] is monotone in (l,r); shrink the lower wall in container problems.

ALGORITHM: two-sum, 3-sum (sort + iterate + two-pointer inside), max area, remove duplicates.

JS-SPECIFIC NOTES: Array.prototype.sort default compare is string-based; pass (a,b)=>a-b for numeric sort.

DRY RUN: twoSumII [2,7,11,15],9 -> [1,2]. 3Sum [-1,0,1,2,-1,-4] -> [[-1,-1,2],[-1,0,1]]. maxArea -> 49.

COMPLEXITY: two-sum O(n); 3Sum O(n^2); max area O(n).
